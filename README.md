# Anderson Energy Lab Manual
This repository stores the lab manual for the Anderson Energy Lab at Cornell BEE. The manual details procedures and expectations for all lab members. It also includes quickstart guides for common lab tasks. Lab and community members are welcome to view the manual, make comments, and propose changes.

## Acknowledgment
This lab manual is forked from the [Srikrishnan Group Lab Manual](https://github.com/srikrishnan-lab/lab-manual) and has been adapted for the Anderson Energy Lab. We are grateful to the Srikrishnan Group for making their manual openly available.
